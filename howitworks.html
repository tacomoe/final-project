FAQs - Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Mentor Fresno?

Mentor Fresno is a community powered program that connects aspiring people with seasoned professionals. Our mission is to strengthen the community by building relationships between young talent and veteran professionals. We are a nonprofit and rely strictly on volunteers.

Can anyone get a mentor?

Yes! Anyone can get a mentor through Fresno Mentors. The only prerequisite is that you’re over 16 years of age and have a drive to better yourself and your community. It doesn’t matter what skillsets you have, what your goals are, or what experience you have. As long as you have the willingness to learn then you’re set to go.
Please keep in mind that mentorships are first come, first served, and based on availability.

How can I become a mentor?

There are 3 major requirements to becoming a mentor:

1. You must make a significant difference in your community.
Whether you’re a successful business owner. A hotshot professional. A financial wizard. Or simply a zen master with years of wisdom and experience to share with the world - it needs to have positively impacted other people.

2. You have something to offer your mentees.
And we don’t mean anything tangible or financial. Whether it be mentorship, advice, guidance, perspective, ideas, or simply a step in the right direction - mentors drive mentees towards achieving their goals.

3. You want to be a mentor.
It’s that simple. Do you have the passion and the drive to take mentees under your wings? Are you willing to share your golden nuggets of wisdom with aspiring individuals? Does the idea of bettering your community excite you?
If so, then welcome to the team.
