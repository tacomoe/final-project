About Us - Fresno Mentor

Who are we?

Mentor Fresno is a community powered program that connects aspiring people with seasoned professionals. Our mission is to strengthen the community by building relationships between young talent and veteran proffesionals. We are a non profit and rely strictly on volunteers.
